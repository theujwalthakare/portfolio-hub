About This Initiative

The MCA 2025 Portfolio Hub is a student-led initiative to organize and showcase the skills and work of our batch in one accessible place.

Objectives

  • Provide a single portal for faculty and recruiters to explore student profiles.
  • Encourage every student to maintain a professional online presence.
  • Promote collaboration and peer learning within the batch.

Maintainers

This hub is maintained by the Class Representative and a small student committee. Updates are made based on official submissions and consent from students.

Data & Privacy

Only the information provided by students through the official form is displayed here. If any student wishes to update or remove their entry, they can contact the CR or committee.
