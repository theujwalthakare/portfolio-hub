MCA 2025 Portfolio Hub

A central place to explore the skills, projects, and portfolios of MCA 2025 students – for faculty, recruiters, and peers.

Browse Students Register Your Portfolio

Why This Hub?

Central Directory

All MCA 2025 student portfolios in a single, easy-to-share link.

Domain-wise Discovery

Quickly find students based on domains like Web, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and more.

Peer Learning

Discover others’ projects, learn from them, and improve your own profile.

How It Works

  1. Build your portfolio using GitHub Pages or any free hosting.
  2. Register your details and links using the Register page.
  3. Your entry appears automatically in the Students list.

Who Can Use This?

  • MCA 2025 students showcasing skills and projects.
  • Faculty reviewing student work and progress.
  • Companies and alumni exploring potential candidates.
