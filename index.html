MCA 2025 Portfolio Hub

A central place to explore the skills, projects, and portfolios of MCA 2025 students – making it easy for faculty, recruiters, and peers to discover talent.

Browse Students Create Your Portfolio

Why This Hub?

One Central Directory

All portfolio links of MCA 2025 students in one place – easy to share with companies, alumni, and faculty.

Tech & Domain Tags

Quickly find students by interests like Web Dev, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and more.

Learn & Improve

Students can see each other’s work, learn from it, and continuously upgrade their own portfolios.

How It Works

  1. Create your portfolio using GitHub Pages or any free hosting.
  2. Fill the official form shared by CR with your details and link.
  3. CR/committee updates this hub with your portfolio entry.

Who Can Use This?

  • MCA 2025 students looking to showcase projects.
  • Faculty members evaluating work and progress.
  • Companies / alumni looking for potential candidates.

Ready to Get Listed?

If you are part of the MCA 2025 batch, build your portfolio and share your link through the official form circulated by the CR.
